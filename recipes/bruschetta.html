Bruschetta Recipe

Description

Italian bruschetta is the best appetizer to start breakfast, lunch or dinner

  • Bread
  • Tomatoes
  • Mint
  • Your favorite spices
  • Olive oil

Preparation

  1. Toast the bread
  2. Chop the tomatoes
  3. Mix chopped tomatoes with your favorite spices
  4. Add oil to tomatoes
  5. Spread the tomatoes on the bread
