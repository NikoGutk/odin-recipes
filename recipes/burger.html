Burger Recipe

Description

Today, we are going to cook a delicious burger, this recipe is perfect for every level of cooks, especially for the beginners!

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Minced Meat
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Pickles
  • Lettuce
  • Onion
  • Your favorite sauce

How to cook?

  1. Cut your vegetables
  2. Season your minced meat
  3. Pan-fry your meat
  4. 1 minute before meat is cooked, add cheese on top of the burgers
  5. Pan-fry your buns
  6. Add sauce to your buns
  7. Compose your burger in a way you like it
  8. Enjoy your burger!
